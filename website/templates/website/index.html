What is Pokedex?

The Pokedex is a digital encyclopedia created by Professor Oak as an invaluable tool to Trainers in Pokemon world. It gives information about all pokemons in the world that are contained in its database. It is an important item in the game, and one of the goals in the game is to complete the Pokedex. In this website, you can find pokemon like real Pokedex!
